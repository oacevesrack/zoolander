IMAC Banner Examples and Usage

Banners

Our banners are the leading graphic element on all pages and therefore one of our largest vehicles for our brand on the page.

These banner types should be used on IMAC refresh campaign pages.

Additional Photographic Requirements for Web Banners:

  • To be used full width so at least 2000k pixels wide - larger if possible.
  • The subject or person is off to the right to not interfere with left text
  • No outdated technology
  • High Quality
  • Full Color (not black and white)
  • Doesn't contain any predominant non-rackspace logos
Large Refresh Banner

This banner should be used for headlines between 70 and 88 characters long. The image area is larger and the box width is wider to accommodate for longer headlines.

Do not use this banner for shorter headlines in order to have a larger banner image area. The banner is dictated by headline length/size.

we guarantee a 99.99% openstack API uptime.
And yes, that .01% does keep us up at night

Medium IMAC Banner

This banner should be used for headlines between 50 and 70 characters long. The image area and box width is medium-sized to accommodate for moderate headlines.

the cloud is a mystery wrapped in a riddle.
We can unwrap it for you

Small IMAC Banner

This banner should be used for headlines under 50 characters long. The image area and box width is small to accommodate for our much shorter headlines.

Do not use this banner for larger headlines in order to have a smaller banner image area. The banner is dictated by headline length/size.

The cloud runs on its own,
with lots of help
