Iconography

Our icons help us to express the attributes of our brand, products, services, workloads, technology and ideas in ways that are immediately recognizable.

We use icons as a graphical representation of a product, service, workload, technology or idea. They should not stand alone. instead, they should be accompanied by a headline or body copy, and the corporate logo should appear somewhere on the same page or asset.

Rules
  • icons are one color and used in dark, medium and light gray.
  • An icon should be used in the context of an idea, service offering, solution area or workload where possible (paired with solution area/workload names, part of an ideogram, diagram or illustration).
  • Make sure that all icons used together have the same line weight.
Usage:

in order to use the Rackspace web font in your markup you will need to put in <span class="rsweb name-of-icon"></span> replacing "name-of-icon" with the name that appears underneath the icon you've chosen.

To change the font color to white add the class "white" to your icon class.

Example
<span class="rsweb name-of-icon white">
